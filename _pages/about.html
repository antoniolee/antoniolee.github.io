Yo!

My name's Antonio Lee. Some people call me ant, twon, or antwon. You can choose whatever you want. I was born and raised in San Jose, California, and I'm still here. Not sure what I think about that, must be because of the nice weather and beautiful sunsets. I've also literally been on the computer since I was in diapers.

I love creating interactive experiences, storytelling, skateboarding, music, video games, learning, teaching, cooking, and watching the sunset. My style is influenced by skate, music, and street culture. Video games play a big part for my love of storytelling, usability and interactive experiences.
