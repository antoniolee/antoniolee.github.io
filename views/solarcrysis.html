Solar Crysis

/ ˈmo͞odē /

PC Video Game

Solar Crysis is a fast paced arcade shooter where the player chooses to defend themselves or repair the solar panel they reside upon. As the solar panel degrades, the level changes dynamically making to harder for the player to survive. This game can be played with up to four players, both versus and cooperatively.

The Setting: Poor Timmy's life is in your hands! He has been put on life support and needs his solar panel to make it through one more week. You control a nanobot whose job is to constantly repair a solar panel that's on the verge of destruction! You must manage repairing the solar panel while protecting yourself from bad guys to ensure Timmy's safety!
