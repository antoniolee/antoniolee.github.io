Falmouth

/ fäl - mūh - th /

AAA Video Game Menu Redesign

Falmouth was a menu re-design for a AAA video game (Disclosed due to NDA). I created designs by reiterating on (disclosed topic). The goals were to increase ease of use, ease of learning, and enhance overall gameplay experience. The project was split into three phases: Discovery, Ideation, and Design Production. Project was achieved by creating a design that decreases number of screens and number of button presses, while effectively hitting all of the goals.

Phase 1: Discovery (Research and Investigation)

•  Researched and mapped out menu navigation for past titles
•  Investigated why certain designs were successful and why others were not, from both a designers and gamers perspective
•  Identified any similarities, differences and unique features across franchise titles and other similar titles

•  Created five unique menu redesigns
•  Created wireframes for each menu redesign
•  Client chooses to pursue with one of the five designs

Phase 3: Design Production (Diverge and Converge)

•  Created two iterations of pursued redesign
•  Developed iterations further with specific client requests
•  Merged both designs to create one design with all desired features.
•  Delivered high fidelity wireframes of final menu redesign
