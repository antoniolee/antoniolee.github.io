Falmouth

/ fal-muh th /

AAA Video Game Menu Redesign

Falmouth was a re-design for one of the world's biggest AAA video games (Disclosed due to NDA). I created designs by reiterating on (disclosed topic). The goals were to increase ease of use, ease of learning, and enhance overall gameplay experience. The project was split into three phases: Discovery, Ideation, and Design Production. Project was achieved by creating a design that decreases number of screens and number of button presses, while effectively hitting all of the goals.

Phase 1: Discovery (Research and Investigation)

•  Researched and mapped out menu navigation for past titles
•  Investigated why certain designs were successful and why others were not, from both a designers and gamers perspective
•  Identified any similarities, differences and unique features across all titles and other similar titles

Phase 2: Ideation (Brainstorm and Rapid Wireframing)

•  Brainstormed ideas from research and sketched out possible redesigns
•  Created five unique and different possible redesigns
•  Created wireframes for each of the five different possibilities
•  Presented redesigns to client; Client chooses to pursue with one

Phase 3: Design Production (Diverge and Converge)

•  Divergence: Created two iterations of pursued redesign
•  Developed chosen iterations further with specific client requests, discuss which features of both designs are desired.
•  Convergence: Merged both designs to create one design with all desired features.
•  Presented final redesign complete with all bells and whistles.
