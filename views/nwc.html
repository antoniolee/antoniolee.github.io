Now We're Cookin'

/ nou - (wir,wər) - ko͝okiNG /

Casual Mobile Video Game

Now We're Cooking is a mobile game for Android and iOS devices. The story takes place in a cooking-reality television show where three hot upcoming new chefs, Breakfast, Dinner and Dessert, have been invited to compete. However when they arrive, the game host announces his plan to trap them and force them to create evil living food creatures forever! Now you must utilize the three different chef powers to turn the evil food back into edible dishes and stop the game host before its too late!

The goal is to cook as much food as possible. At the start of each level, the host will tell you your objectives. Once the level begins, the food that will move towards the chefs. You will be able innovate and create new dishes by swiping together different foods from the three different lanes.
