Now We're Cookin'

/ nou - (wir,wər) - ko͝okiNG /

Casual Mobile Video Game

Now We're Cooking is a mobile game for Android and iOS devices. The story takes place in a reality-cooking television show where three upcoming chefs, Breakfast, Dinner and Dessert, have been invited to compete. However when they arrive, the game host reveals his diabolical plan to use the chefs to create food monsters! You must utilize the three different chef powers to turn the evil food back into edible dishes and stop the game host before it's too late!

The goal is to cook as much food as possible. At the start of each level, the host will tell you your objectives. Once the level begins, the food will move towards the chefs on conveyor belts. You will be able innovate and create new dishes by swiping different foods into each other from the three different lanes.
